About Me

From an early age I was interested in breaking things into their working parts and putting them back together again. Building has always been at the core of my spirit, whether it was a plate of food or my own desktop computer. Versed in full-stack web development including Javascript, SQL, and the MERN stack, I'm now looking to move into a role in which I can expand my knowledge and add more technologies to my tool belt.
I’ve done some truly unique things in my time as a chef and would like to continue the trend of interesting and engaging work. I’m looking for an entry-level position in which I can learn and grow while entertaining my creative side.
